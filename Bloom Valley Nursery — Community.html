Community Involvement

Local School Garden Program

We partnered with Bloom Valley Elementary School to donate seedlings and potting soil for their outdoor classroom garden. Students learned to grow vegetables and appreciate nature firsthand.

Annual Plant Fair

Each spring, Bloom Valley Nursery hosts a free community plant fair where residents can swap cuttings, learn about native species, and connect with fellow gardening enthusiasts in the area.

Events and Collaborations

Stay tuned for information about upcoming events and how to stay in touch. We are always excited to connect with our community and announce new collaborations with local artists, environmental groups, and neighbourhood organizations.

We are thrilled to announce our upcoming participation in the Bloom Valley Harvest Festival, where we will showcase our seasonal plants and offer live potting workshops for all ages.

Additionally, we are collaborating with a local landscape designer to offer free garden planning consultations for first-time homeowners throughout the spring season.
